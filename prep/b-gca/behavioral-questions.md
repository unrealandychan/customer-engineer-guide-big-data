# Interview B — GCA: Behavioral Question Bank

20 real questions drawn from Google interview reports. Organized by theme so you can map your STAR stories efficiently.

**Rule:** Every answer uses STAR + the Lessons line. Every result must contain a number.

---

## Theme 1: Problem-Solving Under Pressure

1. Tell me about a time you faced technical and people challenges simultaneously.
2. Tell me about a time you led a team through a difficult situation.
3. After 6 months, you realize during a meeting that another team is working on the same project. What did you do?
4. Tell me about a time when you worked on a project with a very tight deadline.
5. What happened when a critical feature didn't work on launch day — what did you do?

**What Gene is probing for:** structured thinking under stress, ownership, not panicking without data.

---

## Theme 2: Influencing Without Authority

6. How do you sell an idea to senior management — if you use slides, what's in them?
7. Tell me about a time you convinced engineers to implement a particular feature they resisted.
8. Tell me about a time you handled a difficult stakeholder.
9. Tell me about a time you demonstrated leadership even though you weren't the formal manager.
10. Tell me about a time you had to manage upward — your manager was wrong about something important.

**What Gene is probing for:** CE role is 80% influence-based. No direct authority over customer teams. Can you persuade technically credible people?

---

## Theme 3: Data-Driven Decisions

11. Tell me about a time you made a decision based on data and were ultimately wrong.
12. Tell me about how you used data or analytics to influence a business decision.
13. Tell me about a time you had to make a decision with incomplete data.
14. Tell me about a time when your analysis changed someone's mind at scale (exec, customer, partner).

**What Gene is probing for:** Data literacy, intellectual honesty, ability to say "I was wrong because the data showed X."

---

## Theme 4: Project Ownership & Delivery

15. Tell me about a time you were the end-to-end owner of a project.
16. Tell me about a time you brought a data pipeline/product/platform to production.
17. Tell me about a time you created something from nothing.
18. Tell me about a time you improved a process at a previous workplace.

**What Gene is probing for:** CE specialists must own the full technical solution cycle — from problem diagnosis to production. No half-finished prototypes.

---

## Theme 5: Resilience & Adaptability

19. Tell me about a time you failed and what happened.
20. Tell me about a time you had to motivate a team after a demoralizing event.

**What Gene is probing for:** Google values people who learn fast, not people who don't fail.

## Quick-Prep: 3 Questions to Practice Cold Today

Pick 3 from this list and answer them out loud WITHOUT looking at your notes. Time each answer:

- "Tell me about a time you faced technical and people challenges simultaneously."
- "Tell me about a time you had to make a decision with incomplete data."
- "Tell me about a time you were the end-to-end owner of a project."

Target: 3.5 minutes per answer. Lessons line must be present. One number must be in the result.
